Cover Art

Brief

To create cover art for a lookbook featuring a jewlery collection. The collection was themed on the god Ganesha, as well as forest elements.

Symbolism

In order to pay homage to the god, the symbol of a mouse and laddoo was used, both of which are traditionally associated with Ganesha. These symbols can be found hiding subtly in the central opening of the foliage.

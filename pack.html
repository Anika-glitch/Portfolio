Packaging Design

Brief

Take a pre-existing biscuit packaging which has a low price point, and increase the products value just by changing the external packaging.

Execution

The product used was Parle Happy Happy Biscuits, who's target audience is children. The original packaging is quite standard and colourful. In order to increase it's value this standard package was converted into a playful package for gifting. The body was enlarged and decked with leaves and character elements.

Context

This upgraded package could be kept in Airports or gift shops as a quick gifting solution for children.

Book Cover Design

Brief

Re-design one book cover that already exists. In the same theme, design the cover of a hypothetical complementary book to be read along with it.

The Theme

Other worlds that examines the possibility that the universe consists of multiple dimensions, hence the visuals reflect the theme of transcending dimensions with geometric shapes. The second book is an educational book about a mathematician who studied dimensionality in cubes extensively, the cover reflects this accordingly.

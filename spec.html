Speculative Design - Interactive advertising

Concept

As brands strive for deeper connections with their customers, it is possible that the future will call for hyper personalisation while doing away with old practice of celebrity endorsements.

Goal

A design that allows for every customer to be the face of their favorite brand.

How it would work

As an individual walks up to the advertising screen, a few visual features of the persons face and body are picked up by the screen. The screen then recalibrates the avatar in accordance with these new features.

Context

This speculation has been designed in the context of around 5 years in the future. The hypothetical brand is a confectionary product named Cosmic Candy.

Time+Tools

3 days

Explanation

The key color worn by the passerby is blue. Their main features that stand out are their glasses and a braided hairstyle.

The screen recognises these traits and changes its avatar. The new avatar now depicts a dominant blue colour palette, along with glasses and a braided hairstyle.
